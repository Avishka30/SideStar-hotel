About SideStar Hotel

Nestled in the heart of the city, SideStar Hotel is your perfect retreat for comfort, elegance, and a memorable experience.

Founded in 1998, SideStar began as a cozy family-run inn with just 10 rooms and a dream to offer unmatched hospitality. Over the past 25 years, we’ve grown into one of the city’s most trusted hotels, offering a range of luxurious accommodations, personalized service, and warm, welcoming experiences that keep guests coming back year after year.

Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, our team is here to make your stay relaxing, enjoyable, and tailored to your needs.

Our Story

SideStar started with a single goal: create a hotel where guests feel like family. In our early days, we focused on quality over quantity, ensuring that every guest was treated with care and attention.

As our reputation grew, so did we. We've expanded to over 80 rooms, added spa services, rooftop dining, and modern amenities — all while keeping our original charm and dedication to service alive.
